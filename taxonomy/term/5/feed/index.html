Being issued by the FAO, it is likely be widely recognised by policy makers and other stakeholders as holistic global framework fostering partnerships for the long-term transformation of food systems. An adaption of this tool to the specifics of permaculture will be discussed.</span></p> <p><span style="font-size:16px"><strong>Authors</strong>: Fiebrig I, Schmutz U, Wright J, Pimbert M</span></p>
